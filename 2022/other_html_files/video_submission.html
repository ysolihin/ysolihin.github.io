VIDEO SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

 

 

Video Upload Deadline: Nov. 1st, 2021

  1. Upload the video files to the following BOX upload link. Upload Link
  2. Video Requirements: MP4 format, H.264 codec, 1920x1080 resolution (1080p, 16:9 aspect ratio). Please make sure each video is under 800MB.
  3. Long presentation video (20 minutes): This video will be made available to the audience before the conference for offline viewing at any time. This video will be posted on YouTube.
  4. Short presentation video - regular papers (7 minutes). During your conference slot, you are expected to deliver a short 7-minute presentation, followed by 3 minutes of Q&A. If there are technical difficulties while trying to deliver your presentation live, we will use the video instead.
  5. Short presentation video - best paper nominees (12 minutes). During your conference slot, you are expected to deliver a short 12-minute presentation, followed by 3 minutes of Q&A. If there are technical difficulties while trying to deliver your presentation live, we will use the video instead.
  6. To submit your videos, please name your files using the following format (all lowercase, where paperid is the HotCRP submission ID of the paper):
    * [paperid]_[last_name]_long.mp4 for the long presentation video
    * [paperid]_[last_name]_short.mp4 for the short presentation video

Questions? Feel free to contact the Virtual Arrangements Chairs via email, Dimitrios Skarlatos: dskarlat@cs.cmu.edu, and Qiuyun Llull: qiuyun.llull@gmail.com.
